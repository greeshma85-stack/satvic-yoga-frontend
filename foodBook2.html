This book contains over 70 new plant-based Satvic recipes curated by the Satvic Movement Culinary Team.

The recipes include healthy salads, nourishing soups, and celebratory dishes like dahi vada, malai kofta, dum aloo, papdi chat, nachos and pizza.

All recipes are free from refined oil, sugar, pungent spices, milk & milk products and processed foods. They are made using ingredients straight from nature, the way you find them in nature.

Food Book 2 is packed with beautiful photography, delicious recipes and simple health knowledge!

  • 9 concise Satvic Food Laws (rules to remember while Satvic cooking)
  • Detailed Guide to setup your Satvic Kitchen (shopping lists, equipment needed)
  • Updated Meal Plans for people wanting to follow the Satvic Lifestyle
