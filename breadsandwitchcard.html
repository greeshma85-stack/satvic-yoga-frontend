5 reasons these dishes are unique

1. No Refined Oil

Only unprocessed, natural fats or cold-pressed oils.

2. No Refined Sugar

Naturally sweetened with dates, raisins, and jaggery.

3. No Refined Flour

Made with whole grains, rich in fiber and nutrition.

4. 100% Plant-based

Completely free from meat, fish, eggs, and dairy.

5. No Processed Ingredients

Nothing canned, bottled, or loaded with chemicals.

FAQs

Will the session be live or pre- recorded?

To provide the best and most engaging experience, the session will be conducted live, not pre-recorded! You'll be cooking along with us in real time, right from your kitchen, alongside hundreds of other participants.

Is this a cook-along workshop, or can we cook the food later?

This is a cook-along workshop, meaning you will be preparing the dishes together in real-time. It is recommended to be in your kitchen during the workshop time to fully participate and make the most out of the experience.

Will recordings be provided? I'm in a different country/time zone.

Recordings of the sessions will be made available for 3 days

How will the sessions be hosted?

The course will be held on the Satvic App. We'll share all the details on how to access it with you before the workshop starts.

About The Hosts

Mini Gupta

Mini is a satvic culinary educator who has dedicated her life to sharing the transformative power of satvic cuisine. Trained in the art of raw food preparation in Ubud, Bali, and having volunteered at a

farm-to-kitchen cafe in Auroville, Mini brings a wealth of experience to her craft. She has hosted multiple online and offline satvic food workshops, positively impacting the lives of thousands of individuals. Mini has also developed multiple recipes for the renowned Satvic Food Books. Driven by her genuine desire to bring about profound transformation, Mini envisions a world where individuals can live medicine-free lives.
