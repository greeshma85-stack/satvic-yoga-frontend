FAQs

What if I can't wake up at 5 am everyday?

Don't worry if you can't wake up at 5 AM every day! Our workshop is designed to provide you with the inspiration, knowledge, and tools to help you wake up at 5 AM. If you miss a session, don't worry, our community of participants is always available to offer support and guidance through notes and resources. Additionally, we provide a 24-hour recording for each session to ensure you don‚Äôt miss out on any valuable content. We promise that by the end of the workshop, you will achieve more than you ever thought possible. Join us today and start your journey to becoming an early riser!

Will recordings be provided? I'm in a different country/time zone.

We highly encourage you to attend the session at 5:00 AM IST each morning to fully experience the benefits of the workshop. However, we understand that participants may join from various countries and time zones. To accommodate this, a 24-hour recording is provided for your convenience.

Will the sessions be live or pre-recorded?

To provide the best experience possible (without any technical glitches) the sessions will be pre-recorded. Don't worry, the sessions will still be highly interactive as thousands of other participants will be watching the sessions with you. While the sessions are pre-recorded, our coaches and other participants will still be available to answer any questions and provide support through our Facebook group. So, join us for an engaging experience designed to help you wake up early and achieve your goals!

On what platform will this workshop be conducted on?

The course will be held on the Satvic App. You won't need to switch on your camera or mic. We'll share all the details about accessing the course 7 days before it starts.
