Our 7 Core Values

1. Service Before The Self

We put the needs of our community before our own needs. We put service to the community before profits. We never take decisions based on how much money, fame or benefit we will gain. We make decisions based on how much impact weâ€™ll create in the lives our community.

2. Simplicity In Delivery

Our knowledge is communicated with such clarity that even a five-year-old can grasp it effortlessly. We consciously avoid complex jargon and overly technical language. Whether it's our videos, books, social media, even work structure, or clothing, we always strive for simplicity.

3. Profoundness Of Wisdom

Our teachings are deeply profound, rooted in the Vedas and the ancient scriptures. Yet, we also use modern scientific insights to back it up. We do not share anything based on our personal opinions or trends.

4. Practicality Of Teachings

We don't just share 'why' to do something, or 'what' to do. We also always share the 'how'. At the end of any theory, we tell people how to exactly bring it into implementation the very next day. In other words, we lay out a detailed path to follow any teaching in one's day-to-day, everyday life.

5. Under Promising, But Over Delivering

We strive to over deliver in everything we do. Whether we create a design, shoot a video, create a workshop or organise an event, it is done in the highest possible quality, and we strive to do more than what is expected. We never do 'kaam chalau' work.

We use the best quality equipment. There are never any grammatical errors in our writing. There are no ordinary workshops we create. Satvic Movement is known for always going over and above and no one compromises it.

6. Empowerment, Not Dependency

We help our community break free from dependencies. Whether itâ€™s the dependency on pills, on addictive substances, on people, or even the dependency on us. Rather, we teach them how to stand on their own feet.

We donâ€™t just give them a diet plan. We teach them how to craft a diet plan so in the future, they can do it for themselves, even without us. We donâ€™t just tell someone to do a certain action. We tell them exactly WHY to do it, so they can become their own health guide, and be fully self-sufficient.

7. A Spirit Of Joy

Satvic Movement is a happy place where joy is constant. Whatever we do, we have fun while doing it. In our courses, we add fun elements so the overall vibe is youthful and joyful, and there are no dull moments (kirtans, team dances, quizzes, surprises, gifts, etc). We like to celebrate small achievements. We like to mark milestones. We love celebrating life!
