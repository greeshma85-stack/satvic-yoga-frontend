About Our Program

3 films | 50 mins each

Film 1

This film empowers children to read ingredient labels and spot chemicals in packaged food.

Film 2

The second film exposes the dark reality of the food industry and empowers children to break free from junk food cravings.

Film 3

Our final film makes children fall in love with the power of fruits, vegetables, and home-cooked meals.

FAQs

What age group is this workshop designed for?

The films are ideal for children in Classes 3 to 7 (ages 8 to 13). The content, language, and activities are specially tailored for this age group.

Is this an in-person or online workshop?

This is an online, video-based workshop. Once your school fills up the interest form, we'll provide you with secure video links to stream each session. All sessions are pre-recorded and easy to facilitate.

In which language are the sessions available?

All sessions are in English language

How will we play the sessions in school?

Once your school fills up the interest form, we'll provide you with secure video links to stream each session. You can play the films using a projector, smartboard, or even a TV with internet accessâ€”whatever works best for you. No special software is neededâ€”just a stable internet connection and speakers for audio.

What kind of setup is required?

A basic projector or smart TV with internet access is sufficient. The films can be streamed directly through a secure link provided upon registration.

How many students can watch the film together?

That depends on your school's setup. You can screen the film in your auditorium, AV room, or classroomâ€”whichever space is available. If you have a large number of students, you can also host the sessions in multiple batches to ensure every child gets the full experience without overcrowding.

Do teachers need to be present during the screening?

Yes, we recommend teachers to be present throughout the session to help maintain discipline and provide technical support if needed during the session.
