Shipping policy

Shipping Policy At Satvic Movement, we strive to deliver your orders efficiently and promptly. Below are our shipping guidelines:

Order Processing: All orders are processed within 24-48 hours of receive, excluding weekends and holidays.

Domestic Shipping: All domestic orders are processed and delivered within 7 business days from the date of order received. We offer free shipping anywhere in India

International Shipping: All international orders are processed and delivered within 10-15 business days from the date of order received.

International Shipping: (Customs and Duties) For international orders, please note that: The customer is responsible for any additional customs duties and taxes levied by the destination country. - We are not responsible for any delays or issues caused by customs clearance.

Delivery Exceptions: In rare cases, delivery may be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances, such as: - Inclement weather - Natural disasters - Carrier delays - Customs clearance issues - Area Restrictions /Prohibited Area

Tracking and Delivery: Once your order is shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation via whatsapp, email, Text sms with tracking information. You can use this information to track the status of your order.

Shipping Carrier : Bluedart , DHL, DTDC, Speed Post , etc.. We aim to deliver your orders as soon as possible. If you have any questions or concerns about your order, please don't hesitate to write to us on orders@satvicmovement.
