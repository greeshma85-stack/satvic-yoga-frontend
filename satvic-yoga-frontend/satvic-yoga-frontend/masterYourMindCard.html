What's Included?

Deep Knowledge of the Mind

Understand the mechanics of the mind both from a psychological and yogic perspective. This will give you the power to observe & control the mind.

Daily Movement

Practice 20 minutes of movement to get out of the thinking mind and become more present. This will also release happy hormones in the body & prepare you for kriya practice.

Yogic Kriyas

Learn & Practice ancient yogic tools such as Pranayama, Chanting and Dhyana to manage overthinking, anxiety, stress and depression.

FAQs

Will the sessions be live or pre- recorded?

To provide the best experience possible (without any technical glitches) the sessions will be pre- recorded. Don't worry, the sessions will still be highly interactive as thousands of other participants will be watching the sessions with you. While the sessions are pre-recorded, our coaches and other participants will still be available to answer any questions and provide support through our Facebook group. So, join us for an engaging experience designed to help you wake up early and achieve your goals!

Can complete beginners join this workshop?

Yes, absolutely! The tools taught are simple for everyone to understand, even without any background in psychology or Yoga. All the practices will be beginner level too!

I'm in a different time zone or country & cannot attend the sessions LIVE. How can I join?

The replay link will be available for 24 hours. You can attend the session using the recording during this time.
