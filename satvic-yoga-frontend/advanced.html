FAQs

Can beginners join this class?

No, this class is for those who have attended the 21 Day Yoga Sadhana Beginner and the 14 Day Yoga Sadhana Intermediate Program with Satvic Movement.

What is the fitness level required for this workshop?

A good level of fitness is required for this workshop as it involves advanced asanas that are done without many breaks in between.

I am in a different time zone/country and cannot attend the live sessions. Will the sessions be recorded?

Yes! The replay links of each session will be made available for 24 hours. You can use them to join the advanced program.

Can I practise Yoga during pregnancy?

Yoga is very beneficial for pregnant women. However, the asanas need to be planned based on the specific trimester. Since we are hosting this workshop online, we are not equipped to provide one-on-one guidance for pregnant women. Therefore, it would be best for pregnant mothers to avoid this workshop.
