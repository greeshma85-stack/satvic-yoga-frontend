From the second week onwards, we‚Äôll shift focus to breath and alignment, and only in the third week will we move towards flows. The workshop is suitable for all levels.

The sessions will be pre-recorded to ensure a seamless webinar experience. Additionally, the teachers will provide real-time support through the Facebook group.
