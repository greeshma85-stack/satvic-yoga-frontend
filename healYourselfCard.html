FAQs

How will the sessions be hosted?

The course will be held on the Satvic App. You won't need to switch on your camera or mic. We'll share all the details about accessing the course 7 days before it starts.

Who should NOT enroll in this challenge?

There are certain health conditions for which this program may not be enough. These include conditions such as:- Vitilig- IBS- CKD- Rheumatoid arthritis- Ankylosing spondylosis- Osteoporosis- Alopecia- Liver cirrhosis- Cancer- Muscular dystrophy - Type 1 diabetes- Hashimoto's thyroid- Clinically diagnosed depression or neurological/psychiatric disorders. For the above mentioned conditions, the Satvic healing plan could be helpful, but may not be sufficient on its own. These conditions often require adjunct therapies and personalized guidance from a certified doctor, which we are unable to provide in an online platform like Satvic Movement. Additionally, there are certain people for whom this program is simply NOT designed for. These include: - pregnant or breastfeeding mothers- children below 14 years of age- individuals who are severely emaciated (extremely underweight) or dealing with anorexia. Please do not join this course is you fall in any of these categories.

Will recordings be provided? I'm in a different country/time zone.

Yes, we will be providing 24-hour access to the session recordings. If you're unable to attend the live sessions, you can watch the recording at your convenience anytime within 24 hours after the session. This way, you can easily stay on track with the challenge.

What if I miss a session from the 7 days?

We encourage you to try your best to join all the sessions, as you'll experience the real benefit by participating fully. However, if you miss a session, don't worry! We provide 24-hour access to the session recordings. Additionally, detailed notes for each session will be available on our app. You can watch the recording at your convenience and attend the next session on time to stay on track.
